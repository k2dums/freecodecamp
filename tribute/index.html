Alan Turing

Memory Down the Lane:

  • 1912 - Born in Maida Vale,London,England.
  • 1934 - Graduates with distinction in Mathematics from King's College,Cambridge.
  • 1938 - Goes to Princeton University in America to study Mathematics and gets awarded PhD.
  • 1939 - Asked to join the Government Codes and Ciphers School
  • 1940 - With Gordon Welchman,develops the Bombo tho decipher messages sent by the Germans
  • 1945 - At the end of war,awarded the OBE for his wartime service
  • 1946 - Publishes with detailed design of a stored program of computer
  • 1951 - Elected Fellow of the Royal Society
  • 1952 - Is arrested
  • 1954 - Dies in his home in Wilmslow,Cheshire,from poisoning

"A computer would deserve to be called intelligent if it could deceive a human into believing that it was human."

--Alan Turing
